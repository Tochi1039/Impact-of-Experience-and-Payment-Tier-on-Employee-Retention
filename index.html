Impact of Experience and Payment Tier on Employee Retention

Question 1: How strongly do Payment Tier and Experience in Current Domain correlate with the likelihood of an employee leaving (Leave or Not)?

Analysis:

Calculate correlations between variables.

In[3]:
import pandas as pd
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt
import seaborn as sns

# Load dataset
df = pd.read_csv("Employee.csv")

# Calculate correlations
corr_matrix = df[['PaymentTier', 'ExperienceInCurrentDomain', 'LeaveOrNot']].corr()

# Create heatmap
plt.figure(figsize=(8, 6))
sns.heatmap(
    corr_matrix,
    annot=True,
    cmap='coolwarm',
    vmin=-1,
    vmax=1,
    linewidths=0.5,
    fmt=".2f"
)
plt.title("Correlation Between Payment Tier, Experience, and Employee Attrition", pad=20)
plt.xticks(rotation=45)
plt.yticks(rotation=0)
plt.savefig('correlation_btw_payment_tier_experience_and_employee_attrition.png')
plt.show()

Interpretation:

Payment Tier vs. Attrition (LeaveOrNot)

Correlation coefficient: -0.15 (weak negative correlation).

Lower payment tiers (Tier 3) correlate with slightly higher attrition.

Experience vs. Attrition

Correlation coefficient: -0.03 (negligible correlation).

Experience in the current domain has almost no impact on retention.

Payment Tier vs. Experience

Correlation coefficient: -0.01 (no meaningful relationship).

# Bin experience into groups
df['ExperienceGroup'] = pd.cut(
    df['ExperienceInCurrentDomain'], 
    bins=[0, 2, 5, 10], 
    labels=['0-2', '3-5', '5+']
)

# Attrition by experience group (with observed=False)
attrition_exp = df.groupby('ExperienceGroup', observed=False)['LeaveOrNot'].mean().reset_index()

# Line chart
plt.figure(figsize=(8, 6))
plt.plot(attrition_exp['ExperienceGroup'], attrition_exp['LeaveOrNot'], marker='o', color='purple')
plt.xlabel('Experience Group')
plt.ylabel('Attrition Rate')
plt.title('Attrition Rate by Experience Group')
plt.grid(True, linestyle='--', alpha=0.7)
plt.savefig('attrition_rate_by_experience_group.png')
plt.show()

Insight:

Employees with 5+ years of experience have the lowest attrition (18%).
